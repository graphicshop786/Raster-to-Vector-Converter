How to Use

Our discount calculator offers two main functions:

  1. Single Item Discount:
    • Enter the original price
    • Enter the discount percentage
    • Click Calculate to see the discounted price and savings
  2. Bulk Discount:
    • Enter the price per item
    • Enter the quantity
    • Enter the bulk discount percentage
    • Click Calculate to see the total savings and final price per item
