About Percentage Calculations

This calculator helps you with three common percentage calculations:

  • Finding a percentage of a number (e.g., 15% of 200)
  • Finding what percentage one number is of another (e.g., 30 is what percent of 150)
  • Calculating percentage change between two numbers (e.g., price increase/decrease)
