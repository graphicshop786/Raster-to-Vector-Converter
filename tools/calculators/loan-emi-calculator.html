How to Use

To calculate your loan EMI (Equated Monthly Installment):

  1. Enter the loan amount you wish to borrow
  2. Input the annual interest rate (in percentage)
  3. Specify the loan term in years
  4. Click "Calculate EMI" to see your monthly payment and other details
Note: This calculator provides an estimate. Actual EMI may vary based on additional fees and exact interest calculation method used by your lender.
